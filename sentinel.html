Security & FAQ

Is my data safe?

Sentinel-X runs locally in your container. We only receive metadata about findings (waste amount and resource types) to generate your report. Your credentials never leave your machine.

What access is required?

The tool only requires ReadOnlyAccess. It cannot perform write operations, delete resources, or modify your infrastructure in any way.

What happens after payment?

You will be redirected to a secure dashboard to download your PDF Forensic Report and a custom CLI script to automate the cleanup of all discovered waste.

Can I run this in Production?

Yes. Sentinel-X is built to be lightweight and non-intrusive. It uses official AWS APIs with zero impact on the performance or stability of your production environment.
