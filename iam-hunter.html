Security & FAQ

How are my credentials handled?

IAM Hunter operates under Hardened by Design principles. Your AWS keys are used locally within the Docker runtime and never transmitted. Only encrypted metadata about policy violations is synced.

What does the scan cover?

We audit MFA status, cross-account trust relationships, unused access keys, and complex privilege escalation paths (Shadow Admins) that standard scanners often overlook.

Is this SOC2 compliant?

Yes. The resulting forensic dossier maps your identity findings directly to SOC2 Control Frameworks, providing a ready-to-use evidence document for your auditors.

Can I automate remediation?

The Premium Report includes a Hardened-IAM.sh script to automatically revoke over-privileged permissions and enforce MFA across all detected vulnerable accounts.
