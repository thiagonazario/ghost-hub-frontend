Security & FAQ

How are my credentials handled?

IAM Hunter operates under Hardened by Design principles. Your AWS keys are used locally within the Docker runtime and never transmitted.

What does the scan cover?

We audit MFA status, cross-account trust relationships, unused access keys, and complex privilege escalation paths.
