Security & FAQ

What is a Bayesian Risk Model?

Unlike static scanners, Ghost Armor uses probability models to calculate the likelihood of a lateral movement after a breach, prioritizing vulnerabilities that actually lead to critical data.

Does it perform active Port Scanning?

Ghost Armor analyzes Security Groups and VPC Flow Logs metadata. It maps open paths without sending a single packet to your instances, ensuring zero impact on production performance.

Can it detect "Shadow" resources?

Yes. By cross-referencing Route53 records with Load Balancer configs, we identify "Ghost Gateways" and abandoned endpoints that are still publicly accessible.
