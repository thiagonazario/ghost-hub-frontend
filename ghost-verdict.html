                 Hardened FAQ
            

            

                

                    
Is my JSON stored?

                    
No. Ghost-Verdict operates under a Stateless Protocol. Your JSON is processed in memory to generate the hardened version and then immediately purged from our Bunker nodes.

                

                

                    
Why Harden IAM?

                    
Over-privileged accounts are the #1 entry point for ransomware. Reducing wildcards (*) limits the blast radius of any potential breach.

                

                

                    
SOC2 & HIPAA Ready?

                    
The output policies follow the Least Privilege principle required by most security compliance frameworks including SOC2 and HIPAA.

                

                

                    
Multi-Cloud Support?

                    
Current version is optimized for AWS IAM. Support for Azure RBAC and GCP IAM is under active Bayesian training.
